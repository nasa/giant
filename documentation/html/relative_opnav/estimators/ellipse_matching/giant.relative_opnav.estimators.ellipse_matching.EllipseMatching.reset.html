EllipseMatching.reset¶

giant.relative_opnav.estimators.ellipse_matching:

EllipseMatching.reset()[source]¶

This method resets the observed/computed attributes, the details attribute, and the limb attributes to have None.

This method is called by RelativeOpNav between images to ensure that data is not accidentally applied from one image to the next.
